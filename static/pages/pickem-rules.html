Pick’em Pool Official Rules

Prizes
  • 1st Place: $500
  • 2nd Place: $250
  • 3rd Place: $50

Competition Structure

The Pick’em Pool consists of five (5) categories. Each category will have a first-place, second-place, and third-place finisher.

Scoring System

Points are awarded for each category as follows:

  • Correct 1st Place: 12 points
  • Correct 2nd Place: 7 points
  • Correct 3rd Place: 4 points
  • Exact Order Bonus: An additional 5 points will be awarded if a participant correctly predicts 1st, 2nd, and 3rd place in the exact order within a single category.

Points are cumulative across all five categories.

Tie-Breaker Rules

In the event of a tie in total points, placements will be determined using the following tie-breakers in order:

  1. Most correctly predicted 1st-place selections
  2. Most categories with an exact-order bonus
  3. Most total correct placements (1st, 2nd, or 3rd)
  4. Highest score in the designated tie-breaker category

Unresolved Ties

If a tie remains after all tie-breakers have been applied, the applicable prize money will be combined and split evenly among the tied participants.

Eligibility

All participants must submit their selections prior to the published deadline. No changes or edits to submissions are permitted after the deadline has passed.

Final Authority

All scoring, results, and tie determinations are final. The pool administrator reserves the right to interpret and enforce these rules and to resolve any disputes./p>
